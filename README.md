# ∞ DEEP WORK

> **Study Smarter. Rank Higher.**

India's first competitive study platform built specifically for students preparing for competitive exams — IPMAT, JEE, NEET, UPSC, CAT, GATE and more.

---

## 🚀 What is Deep Work?

Deep Work is a focus study app where Indian competitive exam students can:

- ⏱️ **Track study hours** with a distraction-free focus timer
- 🏆 **Compete on leaderboards** with students preparing for the SAME exam
- 🔥 **Build daily streaks** and stay consistent
- 📊 **Analyse their progress** with personal analytics
- 👥 **Connect with a community** of same-exam aspirants
- 🎖️ **Earn badges and level up** as they study more

---

## 💡 Why Deep Work?

While apps like Forest and Regain focus on blocking distractions, Deep Work focuses on something more powerful — **competition and community**.

> *"While others block apps, Deep Work students top leaderboards"*

No other app in India combines a focus timer with exam-specific leaderboards and communities. Deep Work is built by a student, for students.
